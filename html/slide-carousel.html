Tina's vision is like a Monet painting

"My vision is very unpredictable. It likes to do what it wants. My vision goes from visually impaired to only seeing movement, light, shadows, and color... kinda like Monet paintings."

Sometimes, Omer needs a vision break

"I like to do fun stuff like any other kid. But sometimes I get really tired quickly. If I'm at school, I take a walk or put my head down on the desk for a minute or two. It sometimes helps and sometimes stays the same, but never gets worse."

Aidan likes to paint watercolors

"I paint postcards. It's not easy to do because it's a small little square thing, but it comes out really well... usually! I mostly like painting at my house. I like to paint how I feel."

Color is like a landmark for Micah

"I do well with color. Colors help me distinguish things like cartoon characters that I've seen a bunch of times. I have a mental picture of them in my head so I know how to map them. It's like a landmark."

Kyle calls his white cane an "extender" because it extends his vision

"There are different levels of blindness - just because you see someone with a white cane, [it] doesn't mean they're completely blind."

Zeke likes to tell jokes

"I just am what I am. Sometimes my memories make me embarrassed. [But] I like jokes!"
